The Vision Behind SO Interiors

Sourabh Jangid & Om Prakash

Co-Founders — SO Interiors

SO Interiors was built on a shared belief that interior design should be more than aesthetics — it should be about how spaces function, endure, and make people feel.

Sourabh Jangid brings a design-first approach to every project — defined by clean lines, controlled palettes, and an unwavering commitment to finish quality. With experience across residential, commercial, and hospitality projects, his eye adapts to context while maintaining a consistent design standard.

Om Prakash brings structure and operational excellence to the practice. From vendor coordination and timeline management to cost optimization and quality assurance, he ensures that what gets designed also gets delivered — on time and on budget.

"Good design isn't about adding more — it's about knowing exactly what to keep. And execution is where design becomes reality."

Our Vision

Building a Design Practice That Lasts

We're building SO Interiors to be a practice known for integrity — in our designs, our processes, and our relationships with clients.

Design Integrity

We never compromise design intent during execution. What we present is what we deliver — no shortcuts, no substitutions, no diluted outcomes.

Continuous Growth
