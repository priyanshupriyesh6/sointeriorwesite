About Us

Our Story & Philosophy

At SO Interiors, we believe that every space should be a reflection of the people who use it. Our approach focuses on combining creativity, functionality, and attention to detail to design interiors that are both visually appealing and highly practical.

We work across residential and commercial projects, including homes, offices, cafés, salons, and retail spaces. Each project is handled with a personalized approach to ensure it aligns with the client's lifestyle, purpose, and expectations.

Co-powered by Jangid Interiors, we bring strong execution capabilities along with modern design sensibilities to deliver reliable and high-quality results.
